## Withdrawal Schedule

- Withdrawals requested between Monday 12:05 PM UTC and Thursday 12:05 PM UTC are processed and made available for claiming the following Monday at 12 PM UTC.
- Withdrawals requested between Thursday 12:05 PM UTC and Monday 12:05 PM UTC are processed and made available for claiming the following Thursday at 12 PM UTC.

![](/img/defi-usdt-withdrawal-schedule.png)

## How to Check Your Next Withdrawal Date

To see when your withdrawal will be available:

1. Navigate to the Withdrawal tab in the app.
2. Enter a withdrawal amount.
3. The expected date will be displayed.

The app will always reflect the next upcoming batch date.
